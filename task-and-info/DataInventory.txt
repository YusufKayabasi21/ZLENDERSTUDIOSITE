Project Type: Framer-to-Vercel Migration
Base Architecture: Magnetto Template (FTC Studio)

🎨 1. Visual Identity (Color Palette)
The site follows a "High-Contrast Dark" theme designed to make 3D renders pop.

Element	Hex Code	Purpose
Primary Background	#000000	Main canvas background.
Secondary Background	#0A0A0A	Section backgrounds / Card backgrounds.
Primary Text	#FFFFFF	Headlines and high-priority copy.
Secondary Text	#A1A1A1	Subtext, labels, and paragraph body.
Accent / Glass	rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)	Border/Glassmorphism effect for cards and nav.
Border Color	#1A1A1A	Subtle dividers and grid lines.

🔡 2. Typography (Design System)
Based on the Magnetto design system and the "Zlender" branding choice:

Heading Font (H1, H2, H3): "Clash Display" or "Slender" (Custom/Display Serif).

Note: Given the name "Zlender," it is highly likely they are using a display font with high contrast between thick and thin strokes (Didone style).

Body Font (Paragraphs, UI): "Inter" (Variable).

Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-bold).

Monospace (Metadata/Buttons): "JetBrains Mono" or "SF Mono".

🚀 3. Technical Stack Recovery
Original Engine: Framer (React-based).

Animation Engine: Framer Motion.

Scroll Logic: Native Framer Scroll + Intersection Observer for video triggers.

Media Types: * Backgrounds: .mp4 (H.264/H.265) and .webm.

Static Assets: .webp (optimized for fast loading).

Icons: .svg (vector-based).

🧩 4. Component Mapping
Navigation: Sticky Glassmorphism header. Transitions from transparent to dark-blur on scroll.

Hero Section: Full-screen width. Centered typography over a high-res 3D video loop.

Service Grid: 2x2 or 3x1 grid with "hover-tilt" or "hover-glow" effects.

Showreel: Integrated video player with custom play/pause UI.

Contact: Modern minimal form (to be re-plumbed to Formspree or Getform).
